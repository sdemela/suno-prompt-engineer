FAQ

QUESTIONS.

What is a Suno Style prompt and why does it matter? +
The Style of Music field in Suno is the most powerful parameter for shaping your track's sound. A well-crafted prompt gives Suno precise direction on genre, energy, instruments and texture. A bad prompt produces generic results. Suno Prompt Engineer generates prompts calibrated for each Suno version's specific behavior.
Do I need to create an account? +
No. The tool is completely anonymous. A session ID is generated in your browser automatically. You can optionally save your email to recover credits on other devices.
What happens when my free generations run out? +
The free tier resets at midnight every day. If you need more, you can buy credits once — they never expire and are tied to your session ID, usable on any browser or device.
What's the difference between the Suno versions? +
Each version responds to different prompt styles. v3 needs short keyword lists, v4 and v4.5 work best with layered descriptors, while v5 understands natural language phrases and can handle richer sonic descriptions. Our engine adapts automatically.
Can I use the generated prompts commercially? +
Yes. The prompts you generate are yours. Commercial use of Suno-generated music depends on Suno's own terms of service — check their site for the latest policy.
What if I switch browsers or devices? +
Your credits are tied to your session ID. Enter it in the "Restore credits" field on any device to recover them. Save your session ID or link it to an email address for safekeeping.
